    Deleting a Campaign

    The campaign is in DRAFT status.
    You can delete a campaign in the All Campaigns page.
    1. In the Home page, click Home, and then click All Campaigns.
      The All Campaigns page is displayed.
    2. In the Your campaigns section, select the appropriate campaign in DRAFT status, and click .
    3. From the drop-down list, select Delete Campaign.
      A confirmation dialog box is displayed.
    4. Click Yes.
